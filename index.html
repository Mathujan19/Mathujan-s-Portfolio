About Me.

I am Vimalarajah Mathujan, an undergraduate student at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, pursuing a BSc (Hons) degree in Information Systems. My academic journey and personal projects have shaped my passion for technology, creativity, and innovation. I am currently working on projects related to software development and IoT-based automation systems, where I integrate microcontrollers and sensors to build practical solutions. Alongside this, I am strengthening my foundation in data structures, algorithms, and problem-solving. My areas of interest include deep learning, software development, game development, and design. I particularly enjoy exploring how storytelling and technology can be combined to create meaningful user experiences. Looking ahead, my ambition is to become an entrepreneur who builds impactful solutions and contributes to the tech industry through innovation, creativity, and leadership.
